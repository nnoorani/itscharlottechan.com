This is Charlotte.

Charlotte is a student at the University of Waterloo, studying Systems Design Engineering. She loves to design and talk to people. You will generally find her talking about travelling or what to eat for [insert meal of the day]. As a budding entrepreneur, she has been working with various people, building her design and marketing portfolio. If you want to work together, give her a shout!

Charlotte likes to keep busy. Her first job was at age 14 as a camp counsellor at Chu's Martial Arts. Soon, she was deemed the role "Arts & Crafts Director". She has worked in the customer service industry for over 6 years; and loves it. Since then she has worked for companies including Deloitte, Rogers Communications, Kontagent and Loblaws E-Commerce.

Martial artist, rock-climber, dragon-boater, stage performer, model, singer, Waterloo Orientation Leader; these are just a few of the hats she wears.
